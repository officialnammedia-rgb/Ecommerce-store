## SECTION 7 — Product photography ⚠️

Quality photos are the single biggest driver of conversion. Options ranked by quality:

| Approach | Cost | Time | Quality |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Professional shoot** (model + studio) | ₹15k–50k for full catalog | 1–2 weeks | Best |
| **Flat-lay studio shoot** (no model) | ₹5k–15k | 3–5 days | Good |
| **Phone shoot at home** with a white sheet + window light | Free | 1 day | Acceptable for v1 |
| **AI-generated mockups** | Free–₹1k | Hours | Risky for actual products |

### Photo specs

- **Resolution:** minimum 1200×1500 px (ideal 2000×2500)
- **Format:** JPG or WebP, sRGB color space
- **Background:** consistent across all products (white, off-white, or one neutral)
- **Per product:** ideally 3–6 angles + 1 detail shot + 1 lifestyle shot
- **Naming:** `productslug-1.jpg`, `productslug-2.jpg`, etc.
